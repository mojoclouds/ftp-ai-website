The Strategy

What Does "Fade the Public" Mean?

When the majority of bettors pile onto one side, sportsbooks adjust lines ‚Äî creating value on the other side. We find that value.

The Public Bias

Casual bettors are influenced by narratives, brand-name teams, and recency bias. They consistently overvalue favorites, overs, and trending teams ‚Äî creating predictable patterns in the market.

The Contrarian Edge

When heavy public action moves a line, the contrarian side often carries positive expected value. Our models quantify exactly when and where these opportunities exist.

The Pipeline

From Data to Predictions

Every prediction follows a rigorous, automated pipeline.

01

Data Collection

We continuously ingest data from multiple sources: public betting percentages, line movements, team and player statistics, situational factors (rest days, travel, injuries), weather data, and historical matchup performance.

02

Feature Engineering

Raw data is transformed into hundreds of predictive features. We capture momentum, fatigue, public sentiment ratios, line movement velocity, and dozens of sport-specific signals tuned for each league.

03

Model Prediction

XGBoost ensemble models ‚Äî trained and validated on years of historical data ‚Äî evaluate every game. Each model outputs a predicted outcome, confidence score, and expected edge against the current line.

04

Signal Filtering

Not every game has an edge. Our system applies strict confidence thresholds and only surfaces plays where the model identifies a statistically significant advantage ‚Äî typically when public sentiment diverges sharply from model output.

05

Delivery

Qualified predictions are delivered across multiple channels ‚Äî Discord, email, SMS, and our platform ‚Äî with full transparency on confidence levels, model reasoning, and relevant data context.
